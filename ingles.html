Detailed Simulation of On-Premises Service Migration to the Cloud Public

This research project proposes a comprehensive and of this transition, aiming not only to identify its benefits and challenges, but also evaluate the effectiveness of the tools security in protecting data stored in the public cloud.

It is intended to carry out a detailed analysis of this migration, focusing in the critical evaluation of positive and negative aspects, as well as in the effectiveness of commonly used tools, aiming at safety and data protection in the public cloud, in addition to performing analysis of cloud data storage security metrics. To the finalizing this study, we hope to provide not only a more in-depth contribution, but rather contribute to the advancement of knowledge in this crucial field of migration analysis, as well as will also offer valuable insights for businesses, professionals and students interested in understanding and effectively implementing the migration to the public cloud, thus contributing to the advancement of knowledge in this crucial field of cloud computing.
